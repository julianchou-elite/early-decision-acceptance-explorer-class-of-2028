How to read this table

The ED/RD ratio compares the early decision acceptance rate with the regular decision acceptance rate. A higher ratio suggests a larger observed difference, but it should not be interpreted as a guaranteed individual advantage because ED applicant pools may differ substantially by academic strength, institutional priorities, recruited athletes, legacy status, financial aid needs, and other factors.
